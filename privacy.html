GRIPE Privacy Policy

Effective 7 July 2026

GRIPE collects no data. None.

Everything you enter — people, plans, receipts, Explanations of Benefits, claim history, net incomes — is stored on your device and, if you use iCloud, synchronized to the private iCloud database attached to your own Apple Account. Apple's privacy terms govern that storage; we cannot read it, and would frankly rather not.

Specifically, GRIPE has:

  • no server of its own — the app makes no network calls except iCloud sync performed by the operating system;
  • no analytics, telemetry, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs;
  • no accounts, sign-ups, or tracking identifiers;
  • no third parties with whom anything could be shared.

Your health information never leaves your devices and your own iCloud. Deleting the app (and its iCloud data via Settings) removes everything — we hold no copy to delete.

If this policy ever changes, the change will be posted here and noted in the app's release notes before it takes effect.
