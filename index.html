GRIPE

Getting Reimbursed from Insurance Plans, Eventually.

GRIPE tracks extended-health-insurance claims across multiple plans coordinated in sequence. Capture the receipt, file with your first plan, record the Explanation of Benefits, cascade the remainder — and see what's left of every maximum without three portal logins.

Support

Questions, bug reports, or a plan whose booklet defies arithmetic: email gripe@dirtymikeboyz.ca and a human will reply. Include your iOS or macOS version and, if it's a claims question, which plans are involved — never send receipts or personal health information by email.

Plan library

Downloadable coverage grids for real plans — PSHCP, the City, the police, NAV CANADA, Air Canada pilots, and more — transcribed from member booklets and ready to import. Browse the plan library, or contribute your own.

Frequently asked

Where is my data stored? On your device and in your own private iCloud. There is no GRIPE server. See the privacy policy — it's refreshingly short.

Why doesn't GRIPE submit claims for me? No Canadian carrier offers a member-facing claims API, and portal scraping is fragile and against their terms. GRIPE tracks what you file; the filing stays between you and your insurer.

A template's numbers don't match my booklet. Templates are starting points from published booklets, and carriers amend them — every figure is editable under Plans. Your Explanation of Benefits is always the ground truth.
