GRIPE Plan Library

Coverage grids, transcribed once so you don't have to.

Each file below is a .gripeplan — one plan's coverage grid (maximums, co-insurance, shared pools, referral rules) transcribed from the plan's own member booklet. No names, no claims, no health data. The date shown is the booklet edition the grid came from.

These are starting points, not gospel. Carriers amend booklets; group contracts vary. Confirm the figures against your own booklet — GRIPE shows every number so you can check it.

How to use one

iPhone/iPad: tap a file, download it, open it from Files (or the download list) and choose GRIPE — the plan appears under Plans. Mac: download and double-click, or drag the file onto GRIPE's Plans list.

Add your plan to the library

Transcribed your booklet in GRIPE? Export it (Plans → your plan → Export plan file…) and send it in — every plan you contribute spares a coworker the typing.

  • Email the .gripeplan to gripe@dirtymikeboyz.ca with the booklet's name and effective date; or
  • open a pull request on the library's GitHub repository.

Submissions are reviewed by a human before they appear here — that's the whole quality-control department, and it likes booklet page references.
